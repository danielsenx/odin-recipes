Chicken Mushroom and Potato Casserole

Home

Description

This Chicken Mushroom and Potato Casserole is my absolute favourite. Your tongue buds will travel to heaven and back!

Ingredients:

  • Vegetables:
    • 1kg of potatoes
    • 10-12 button mushrooms
    • 1 large onion
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 400g of sour cream
  • 1tbsp Dijon mustard

Steps:

  1. Peel 1kg potatoes and slice them into ⅛ inch slices using a mandolin slicer or a knife. Add salt, freshly ground pepper and garlic powder or ranch seasoning and toss to coat, separating the slices to season evenly.
  2. Cut the chicken breast into small pieces. Add seasoning (salt, black pepper, garlic powder or ranch powder) and toss to coat.
  3. Mix the sour cream with Dijon mustard.
  4. Layer the ingredients in the following order and into many layers:
    1. Add sour cream onto cassserole baking tray.
    2. Layer the potatoes.
    3. Add sour cream on top.
    4. Add meat.
    5. Add sour cream on top.
    6. Add chopped mushrooms.
    7. Add sour cream on top.
    8. Mix in onions.
    9. Repeat until products are finished.
  5. Place into preheated to 200°C oven and bake for 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 180°C and bake for another 40 minutes. Remove the foil or the lid and cook for additional 20-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown. Once out of the oven sprinkle with some cheese (it will melt from the potatoes being hot), chopped chives/green onion & parsley. Serve immediately.
